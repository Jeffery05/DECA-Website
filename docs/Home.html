Welcome to DECA Mackenzie!

As one of Mackenzie's largest and oldest clubs, DECA Mackenzie prepares a specially selected team of 60 members to participate in the regional, provincial and international levels of the DECA competition. Along the way, members become a tight-knit community and develop crucial leadership, public speaking and presentation skills. To learn more, feel free to have a look around our website where you can discover recent announcements, a little bit more about us and photos from past years!
