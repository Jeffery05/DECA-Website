DECA Mackenzie Is BACK!

September 20, 2022

Hellooooo Mackenzie! Someone important once said, "If opportunity doesn't knock, build a door." Well today, DECA Mackenzie is knocking for you. Often described by its members as the pinnacle of their high school experience, DECA is a business competition to help you develop your knowledge and public speaking and make the coolest or wackiest memories and friends. Your journey will begin when you attend our information meeting this Tuesday, Sept. 27 @ 3:30 pm after school in the caf - see you there!
