Community Involvement:

Currently, I am involved in a range of extra-curricular activities both at school and in the community. Through these experiences, I've gained invaluable skills and lessons in responsibility, organization, and leadership. I've also gained a deeper understanding of specific subject areas as well, such as business (DECA), math (Math Club), science (Science Club), and computer science (MCPT). Feel free to have a look!

President - Mackenzie Science Club

As the President of Mackenzie Science Club, I am able to express my passion for STEM through running weekly science events for the club's 200+ members! During the spring, the club also hosts the Mackenzie Science and Engineering Olympics which is a science olympiad that draws over 700 middle school students from all over Ontario! In my role as the President, I am responsible for coordinating events with staff supervisors, student council, members, and a team of 12 executives. Additionally, as the chair of science council, I coordinate collaboration events with 6 other science clubs.

Co-President - DECA Mackenzie

DECA is an international business case competition where students compete in different events to develop their communication, problem-solving and analysis skills. As the Co-President of my school's DECA Chapter, I manage and oversee 10 other executives to help train 60+ members in preparation for the competition.

Assistant Teacher - Spirit of Math

As an Assitant Teacher for the past 3 years, I help lead discussions and engage with students to teach them advanced math concepts during weekly Spirit of Math courses. Additionally, I also help out with administrative tasks and gain experience working in a professional environment.

Executive - Mackenzie Model UN

As an executive for Mackenzie's Model UN club, I help teach lessons and engage with members to prepare them for many Model UN conferences throughout the year.
